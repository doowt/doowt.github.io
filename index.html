About Me

  • Work

    I am a PhD student at the University of Bristol, currently working at KU Leuven, Belgium in the Computer Security and Industrial Cryptography (COSIC) research group. I am supervised by Nigel Smart and am working on privacy-preserving computation, specifically multiparty computation (MPC) and linear secret sharing schemes (LSSSs).

  • Education

    I hold a first class Master of Mathematics (MMath) degree from the University of Warwick and a Diploma of Music Performance (DipLCM) with distinction from the London College of Music.
