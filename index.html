About Me

  • Work

    I am a PhD student at the University of Bristol, currently working at KU Leuven, Belgium. My supervisor is Nigel Smart and I specialise in multiparty computation (MPC) and linear secret sharing schemes (LSSS).

  • Education

    I hold a first class Master of Mathematics (MMath) from the University of Warwick and a Diploma of Music Performance (DipLCM) with distinction from the London College of Music.

  • Not Work and Not Education

    In my spare time, I enjoy playing the piano, doing cryptic crosswords, and worshipping at Kensington Baptist Church, Bristol.
